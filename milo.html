    DEVELOPER FAQ

  • Why Early Access?
    I want to introduce more ways to customize your character and rats (gameplay-wise and cosmetically) as well as create more abilities, power-ups, synergies, and passives that augment each play-through. I also want to add maps, additional weapons and possibly player classes, skill-unlocked cosmetic items/skins, online co-op.
  • Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?
    My plan is to remain in Early Access for approximately one year after launch.
  • How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?
    My goal is that the full version will contain a substantial amount of more maps, gameplay variety, replayability, items/power-ups/synergies, quality-of-life improvements, and graphical/performance improvements.
  • What is the current state of the Early Access version?
    The essential gameplay loop of Milo and the artistic/atmospheric elements of the game are already present.
  • Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?
    I do plan to raise the price when leaving Early Access, and possibly for DLC expansions.
  • How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?
    I currently host a Discord server where players can file bug reports and offer feedback and suggestions.
